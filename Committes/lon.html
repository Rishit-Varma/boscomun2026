About the Committee

Existing currently in 1925. The cannons of the Great War have now fallen silent as well as ignorant, yet the struggle for power has only begun. Across the Near East, the collapse of the Ottoman Empire has left behind disputed borders, imperial mandates, and nations demanding the right to govern themselves. What was promised as peace has become a contest for dominance, where diplomacy masks ambition and sovereignty is weighed against the interests of empires.

The League of Nations now convenes in a Special Session at a defining moment in history. This special session is the battlefield where words may redraw maps, determine the fate of millions, and shape the balance of power for decades to come. Delegates shall represent the world’s most influential statesmen, navigating a maze of secret negotiations, fragile alliances, colonial interests, and rising nationalism.

Within these chambers, every speech carries consequences. Every alliance invites suspicion. Every resolution may preserve peace or just easily sow the seeds of another global conflict. The future of imperial mandates, regional sovereignty, and the Near East now rests in the hands of those bold enough to defend their nation’s interests.

History will never remember the spectators. It will remember people those who had the guts and the boldness to change it.
