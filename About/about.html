Spanning from the fragmented frontlines of the Kosovo War to the disputed lands of the Ogaden War, the Eleventh Edition of Bosco Model United Nations 2026 takes place during a period where to strive for peace requires audacity to break from war. History reverberates in the Hall of the League of Nations as delegates contemplate the uncertain outcomes in the Near East.

Within the halls of Wilhelm’s War Cabinet, the very integrity of Europe hinges on the Ems Dispatch and the territorial integrity of Alsace-Lorraine, where a singular error in judgment may lead to a conflagration of a continent. In the Organisation of Islamic Conference, the delegation will grapple with the challenges and moral quagmires of the Lebanese Civil War where the militarization of Lebanese territories occur within a landscape of regional rivalries.

Each committee will offer an arena for diplomacy to push past boundaries and use the power of human interaction in the face of conflict.
