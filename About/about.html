About BoscoMUN

From the tense chambers of the United Nations Security Council confronting historic geopolitical deadlocks to the League of Nations attempting to preserve global peace, the Eleventh Edition of Bosco Model United Nations 2026 unfolds across a volatile landscape where disruption and diplomacy collide. As Wilhelm’s War Cabinet deliberates on the strategies of global conflict and the Organization of Islamic Conference grapples with regional stability, delegates will be forced to choose between conscience and command.

UNGA-DISEC confronts the shadows of modern militarisation and security threats, with each committee standing as a microcosm of global power, and every delegate carrying the burden of shaping history.
