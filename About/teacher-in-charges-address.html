Teacher-In-Charge's Address

Mr. Debarshi Chakraborty

For over a decade, BOSCO Model United Nations has been a space where young leaders step up, challenge ideas, and find diplomatic solutions to global issues. This year, we are proud to launch our 11th edition under a theme that hits close to home for all of us: "Beyond Conflict."

Today's world is fractured by polarization and unpredictable global crises. Owing to this, true peace requires more than just stopping a fight. It demands creative leadership and the grit to find common ground when differences seem impossible to bridge. "Beyond Conflict" is a challenge to stop treating diplomacy as a panic response to chaos, and start using it to build lasting cooperation and progress.

We have handpicked this year's committees to push your boundaries across both current affairs and historical turning points:

  • UNGA-DISEC & UN Security Council: Navigate the high-stakes complexities of modern international security and global governance.
  • The League of Nations & Wilhelm’s War Cabinet: Step back in time to rewrite history during moments that shaped our world.
  • Organization of Islamic Cooperation (OIC): Drive collaboration and tackle issues of critical regional and global importance.

BOSCO MUN is never just about standard speeches or copy-paste resolutions. It is about sharp strategy, ethical leadership, and the alliances you forge along the way. Every debate is a chance to change your perspective and make your voice heard.

As we embark on the 11th chapter, we want you to think boldly, negotiate fiercely, and lead with integrity. Let's look past the divisions. Let's move Beyond Conflict.

Welcome to BOSCO MUN 2026.
