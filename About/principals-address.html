Rev. Fr. Rector & Principal's Address

REV. FR. V. C. GEORGE, sdb

Greetings to all,

It is with immense pride and heartfelt warmth that I welcome you to Bosco MUN 2026.

Within these halls, may intellect be tempered with humility, conviction with compassion, and diplomacy with integrity. Beyond the resolutions you draft and the speeches you deliver, I hope you discover the enduring value of dialogue, mutual respect, and the courage to embrace perspectives beyond your own.

May Don Bosco Park Circus be more than merely the venue of this conference; may it be a place where you forge lasting friendships, kindle noble aspirations, and leave with memories that endure long after the final gavel falls.

I wish you every success as you embark upon this remarkable journey. May Bosco MUN 2026 inspire not only exceptional debate, but exemplary character.

Best Regards,
Fr. V.C. George, sdb,
Rector and Principal
